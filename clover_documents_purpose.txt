CLOVER STONEFIELD DOCUMENTATION PURPOSE

This project consists of two primary documents:

1. The Saga Document (Adventure Chronicle)
2. The Canon Document (Character Codex)


----------------------------------------
SAGA DOCUMENT (ADVENTURE CHRONICLE)
----------------------------------------

Purpose:
The Saga Document is a structured, chronological record of Clover Stonefield’s adventures as they occur during gameplay.

Objectives:
- Record each adventure segment in a consistent, repeatable format
- Preserve the user’s original narration as the primary source material
- Maintain continuity of events, locations, equipment, and character state
- Provide a clear timeline of Clover’s progression through the world
- Capture commentary and discussion about each segment without overwriting the original narration
- Allow easy reference to past events without rereading large portions of text
- Support long-term expansion across many segments and multiple narrative eras
- Serve as the foundational dataset for potential future narrative generation (e.g., novelization)

Design Principles:
- Minimal manual maintenance
- Consistent structure across all segments
- Scalable to a large number of entries
- Clear separation between narration, commentary, summary, and state


----------------------------------------
CANON DOCUMENT (CHARACTER CODEX)
----------------------------------------

Purpose:
The Canon Document is a stable reference defining Clover Stonefield as a character.

Objectives:
- Establish a fixed, authoritative description of Clover’s personality, behavior, and physical characteristics
- Define how Clover thinks, speaks, reacts, and behaves across situations
- Lock in non-negotiable character traits to prevent drift over time
- Provide a consistent reference for interpreting future events and writing
- Define key relationships (e.g., Clover and Erik) in a stable, reusable format
- Describe Clover’s baseline behavior in ordinary, non-dramatic contexts
- Provide a clear, structured reference for AI-assisted writing and analysis
- Support consistent portrayal across all segments and future generated content

Design Principles:
- Stability over time (not frequently rewritten)
- Clarity and precision over narrative flourish
- Structured, easily scannable sections
- Separation of character definition from story progression
- Explicit constraints to prevent misinterpretation


----------------------------------------
OVERALL PROJECT OBJECTIVE
----------------------------------------

Together, these two documents serve as:

- A living chronicle of Clover’s journey (Saga Document)
- A fixed reference for who Clover is (Canon Document)

This dual-document system ensures that:
- The story can grow freely without losing consistency
- The character remains stable even as circumstances change
- Future AI-assisted narrative generation can rely on both accurate history and accurate characterization

The end goal is to maintain a coherent, scalable, and referenceable record of Clover Stonefield’s full story and identity.
